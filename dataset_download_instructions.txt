In-domain datasets.

SuperGLUE:
Go to https://super.gluebenchmark.com/tasks and click "Download All Data" button at the bottom of the page.

CQA:
Go to https://www.tau-nlp.sites.tau.ac.il/commonsenseqa and click on the "Train", "Dev", and "Test" buttons to download
the data for CQA.

RACE:
Go to https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~glai1/data/race/ and click on the highlighted link.

MPE:
Go to https://github.com/aylai/MultiPremiseEntailment and either download or clone the repository. The data is in the
data folder.

SciTail:
Go to https://allenai.org/data/scitail and click on the blue "Download" button.

Out-of-domain datasets.

SNLI:
Go to https://nlp.stanford.edu/projects/snli/ and click on the download link.

MED:
Go to https://github.com/verypluming/MED and either download or clone the repository.

PIQA:
Go to https://yonatanbisk.com/piqa/ and click on the blud "Download data>>" button.

SIQA:
Go to https://leaderboard.allenai.org/socialiqa/submissions/get-started and click on the download link.

ReClor:
Go to https://whyu.me/reclor/ and click on the download link.

DREAM:
Go to https://github.com/nlpdata/dream/tree/master/data and either clone or download the repository. The data is in the
data folder.

StrategyQA:
Go to https://allenai.org/data/strategyqa and click on the blue "download" button.
Additionally, you can download or clone the following GitHub repository https://github.com/eladsegal/strategyqa
